HyperText Markup Language (HTML)

Definition

HTML is the language used for creating documents on the World Wide Web. It defines the structure of a webpage and the content semantically. Past practices of web development includes specifications for the presentational aspects of a webpage, however, in the present times, it is a good practice to solely use HTML to specify the logical organization of a document and the to hand over presentational aspects to stylesheets.
